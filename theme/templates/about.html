About PyLadies Kuala Lumpur

PyLadies is an international mentorship group focused on helping more women become active participants and leaders in the Python open-source community. Our mission is to promote, educate, and advance a diverse Python community.

Our Mission

In addition to the international mission, PyLadies Kuala Lumpur is committed to empowering women to speak up and confidently share their knowledge and ideas at Python events. We provide a supportive platform for members to exchange ideas, build confidence, and become active contributors and leaders within the tech community.
